KAIRALI CHARITABLE FOUNDATION
(KCF)

Kairali Charitable Foundation was established in 2001 with 21 members under the leadership of Mr.M.V.Parameshwaran(Chairman)

Kairali Charitable Foundation (KCF) was registered officially in 2002(MAH/1338/2002/Pune/Regd No, F359691). KCF supports medical treatments especially for Cardiac, Kidney, Brain and Cancer related problems.We also oraganize medical camps, blood donation camps,provide ambulance services irrespective of caste and creed.We provide necessary assistance and guidance to needy people for getting benefits through Norka Roots (Government of Kerala insurance benefits),Government Aid for education and various other it schemes.

Kairall Charitable Foundation (KCF) has been rendering Yeomon services to the migrant Malayalee Population of Pune and the surrounding areas since the last 17 years. Needless to say, KCF has earned a distinctly trustworthy place of honour among the plethora of Malayalee Organizations in Pune by its sheer dedication to alleviate the sufferings and pain of less fortunate among the migrant Malayalees who have left their kith and kin in search of a living in this far off place. KCF has tried its best to give a helping hand to many in distress thereby wiping out the tears from their eyes. By recognizing and applauding the success of the migrant children in the fields of cultural, social ,educational and scientific fields. KCF has been able to see tears of joy in the eyes of the proud parents and relatives of such talented children. The Malayalee population in and around Pune has been proud to witness these great achievements of the KCF. Even the local population has appreciated the sense of dedication of the team led by Mr. M.V. Parameswaran, the founder Chairman of the KCF.
