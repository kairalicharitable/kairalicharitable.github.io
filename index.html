KAIRALI CHARITABLE FOUNDATION
(KCF)

Kairali Charitable Foundation was established in 2001 with 21 members under the leadership of Mr.M.V.Parameshwaran(Chairman)

Kairali Charitable Foundation (KCF) is a Pune-based Charitable & non-profit organization (Registration: 2002(MAH/1338/2002/Pune/Regd No, F359691). We support medical treatments, especially for Cardiac, Kidney, Brain, and Cancer-related diseases. We also arrange Medical camps, Blood Donation Camps & Ambulance Services irrespective of caste and creed. We provide timely assistance and guidance to those lacking financial support for getting benefits through Norka Roots (Government of Kerala Affiliated Insurance benefits), Government Aid for education, and various other schemes. We have been delivering humanitarian assistance to the migrant Malayalee Population of Pune and surrounding areas for the last 20 years. Needless to say, KCF has earned a distinctly trustworthy place of honor among the plethora of Malayalee Organizations in Pune by its sheer dedication to alleviating the sufferings and pain of less fortunate migrant Malayalees who have left their kith and kin in search of a living in this far-off place.KCF's sheer dedication to them is commendable on regional and state-level platforms. Additionally, we also encourage and applaud the success of migrant children in cultural, social, educational, and scientific fields.Its our privilege to witness tears of joy in the eyes of the proud parents and relatives of such talented children. The Malayalee population in and around Pune has witnessed and appreciated these remarkable achievements of our organization. Even the local population appreciates the sense of dedication of the team led by Mr. M.V. Parameswaran, Founder, and Chairman of KCF. We have tried our best to give a helping hand to many in distress thereby wiping out their tears of sorrow.
