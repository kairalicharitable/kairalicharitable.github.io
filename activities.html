Attukal Ponkala festival at Indrayani Nagar, Bhosari

Attukal Ponkala festival at Indrayani Nagar, Bhosari.was celebrated with lot of Devotees on 17 feb 2022 , Thursday. As usual a Free Medical Camp was arranged at the campus by Kairali Charitable foundation. The Medical Camp was inaugurated On 17 Feb 2022 , Thursday by M.V.Parameswaran, The Chairman of Kairali Charitable Foundation, Pune. The Main Doctor Santosh More Sir and his Colleagues, Staff nurses of Anand Hospital , Bhosari Co-ordinated and co-operated us to complete the camp successfully this year also.

Found after 30 years

Basheer, a native of Arippad, who had not been associated with Kerala since 30 years, contacted Kairali in a critical condition. Kairali activists immediately took necessary medical treatment, saved a life, found the relatives, brought the masked brother Hassan to Pune on 10-2-22 and handed him over to Hassan. Kairali would like to thank the community of well-wishers who helped him.
