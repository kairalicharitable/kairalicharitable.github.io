Jassie Gift
Kairali Charitable foundation Team , Pune wishes Many many happy returns of 27 November in his life. Jassie Gift is an Indian film and music composer and play back singer. He composes songs and sings for Malayalam , Kannada , Tamil & Telugu films. He became famous after the success of his song LAJJAVATHIYE.. from the film 4 the people directed by Jayaraj. He is a Retd Asst. Registrar from Kerala University, Tvm. His Parents are from Aruvikkara, Gift Israel and Rajamma Gift. He completed his studies from Mukkola St.Thomas School & Mar Evanious College. When he was a student he won lots of Awards . He used to work with troops of Western Music ,Tvm. Soona Soona Album fist telecasted by Surya T.V. channel in the media. On 12 Sep 2012, He married Dr.Atulya ,who accomplished a PhD in IT from Kannur University. Kairali Team Congratulates and prays The Almighty to shower blessings on Him today on 27th Nov and stay blessed with success on every step in his life.
